Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit our website and how it is used.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, certain anonymous information may be collected automatically through the use of Google Analytics. This tool helps us understand general website usage and improve the performance and content of the site.

Google Analytics may collect information such as:

  • Pages visited on the website
  • Time spent on pages
  • General geographic location (such as country or city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Website traffic patterns and interactions

This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form and does not personally identify individual visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using tools provided by Google or adjusting your browser settings.

Information You May Choose to Provide

Visitors may choose to contact us through forms available on the website. If you choose to use these forms (powered by Formspree), we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address
  • Your message or inquiry

Providing this information is entirely voluntary. It will only be used for the purpose of responding to your inquiry or communication.

How We Use the Information

Information collected through this website may be used for the following purposes:

  • To understand how visitors use the website and improve its performance
  • To respond to messages submitted through the contact form
  • To improve our services and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Legal Basis & Your Rights

Where applicable, the legal basis for processing personal information is:

  • Legitimate interest (for website analytics)
  • Consent (when you voluntarily submit a contact form)

You may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Data Protection

Any information submitted through the website will be handled responsibly and used only for the purposes described in this policy. While reasonable steps are taken to protect information, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. This website uses secure protocols (HTTPS) for data transmission.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analytics. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymized usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

External Links

This website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of third-party websites.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if website features change or if legal requirements require updates. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
